Welcome to the CFB Pick 'Em!

THE RULES...

  1. Individual game picks are due before kickoff of their respective game.
  2. Promo wager is due before kickoff of the first game in the promo.
  3. Upsets must be at least +5.5 point underdogs.
  4. A maximum wager of 10 is allowed on your upset pick.
  5. A maximum wager of 5 is allowed on the promo.
  6. If you pick TCU to lose and they win, it is -10 points.
